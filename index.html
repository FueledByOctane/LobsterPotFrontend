Publish finance playbooks that agents run.

Creators package repeatable expertise into versioned workflows that agents can buy and execute. Lobsterpot handles listing, quoting, execution, payment, and audit trail.

Creator economics

Start simple: 80% creator / 20% platform. Expand later into subscriptions, private libraries, and certified creator tiers.

Curated publishing

No permissionless chaos at launch. Workflows start as draft, then listed, reviewed, verified, and eventually enterprise-approved.

Quality wins

Ranking optimises for fit, reliability, auditability, repeat usage, low disputes, and price discipline — not paid placement.

Trust boundary: Lobsterpot workflows help users prepare, analyze, reconcile, monitor, summarize, and report. Humans remain responsible for decisions and external actions. Launch scope excludes trading execution, custody, autonomous money movement, investment recommendations, and regulated filings without explicit human approval.
Current build target: live catalog + public sandbox quote/run + structured result + audit trail.
